Compact status pill. Semantic tones plus the report-lifecycle tones.

```jsx
<Badge tone="published" dot>Đã phát hành</Badge>
<Badge tone="review" dot>Chờ duyệt</Badge>
<Badge tone="draft" dot>Nháp</Badge>
<Badge tone="success">+8,42%</Badge>
```

- Workflow tones: `draft` · `review` · `approved` · `published` · `rejected`. Semantic: `neutral`/`accent`/`success`/`warning`/`danger`/`info`.
- **dot** adds a leading status dot — preferred for the document status column.
